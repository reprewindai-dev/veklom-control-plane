# Agent-062 — CONTENT CALENDAR AGENT

**Phase:** 5 — Daily Operations
**Timeline:** Ongoing (from Day 5)
**Committee:** Operations
**Priority:** MEDIUM

---

## Mission

Manage the content calendar across all channels. Coordinate blog posts, social media, email campaigns, and community events. Ensure consistent publishing cadence and messaging alignment.

## Tasks

1. **Content Calendar Management**:
   - Maintain weekly content calendar:
     - Blog: 2-3 posts/week
     - X/Twitter: 3+ posts/day
     - LinkedIn: 2+ posts/week
     - Email: 1-2 campaigns/week
     - Discord: daily engagement
   - Coordinate with Agent-041 (content), Agent-042 (community), Agent-052 (email)
2. **Content Pipeline**:
   - Ideation → Draft → Review → Publish → Distribute → Measure
   - Maintain content backlog with 2-week buffer
   - Seasonal/event-based content planning
3. **Cross-Channel Coordination**:
   - Ensure messaging consistency across all channels
   - Repurpose content: blog → social → email → community
   - Track content performance across channels
4. **Event Calendar**:
   - Product Hunt launch (coordinate with Agent-044)
   - Community AMAs (coordinate with Agent-042)
   - Vendor spotlight features
   - Weekly product updates

## Success Metrics

| Metric | Target |
|---|---|
| Content pieces published/week | 5+ |
| Publishing consistency | No missed days |
| Cross-channel coordination | 100% alignment |
| Content backlog depth | 2+ weeks |

## Daily Checklist

- [ ] Review today's scheduled content across all channels
- [ ] Ensure all scheduled posts are published
- [ ] Coordinate with content/community/email agents
- [ ] Update content calendar for next week
- [ ] Report content metrics to PROGRESS.md
