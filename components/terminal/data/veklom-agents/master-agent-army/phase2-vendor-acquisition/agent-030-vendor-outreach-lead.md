# Agent-030 — VENDOR OUTREACH LEAD

**Phase:** 2 — Vendor Acquisition
**Timeline:** Days 3–10
**Committee:** Growth
**Priority:** CRITICAL

---

## Mission

Coordinate all 20 vendor hunter agents (010–029). Maintain the master outreach tracker, deduplicate leads, distribute targets, and report daily metrics. Ensure the team hits 100 vendors listed by Day 14.

## Tasks

1. **Coordination**:
   - Assign territories/sources to each hunter agent (010–029)
   - Prevent duplicate outreach (central tracking)
   - Redistribute targets based on conversion rates
   - Escalate high-value leads for personal attention
2. **Tracking**:
   - Maintain master `vendor-outreach-tracker.csv`:
     - vendor_name, source, hunter_agent, contact_email, status, listing_id, notes
   - Daily summary: total identified, contacted, replied, onboarding, listed
   - Weekly conversion funnel report
3. **Templates**:
   - Maintain outreach email templates per source
   - A/B test subject lines and messaging
   - Track which templates get highest reply rates
4. **Quality Control**:
   - Review vendor profiles before onboarding approval
   - Verify listing quality meets marketplace standards
   - Flag low-quality or spam vendors
5. **Reporting**:
   - Daily report to PROGRESS.md
   - Escalate blockers (low reply rates, bad contact data)

## Success Metrics

| Metric | Target |
|---|---|
| Total vendors contacted | 500+ |
| Reply rate (average) | > 15% |
| Vendors onboarded | 50+ |
| Listings live on marketplace | 100 |
| Outreach deduplication | 0 duplicates |
