# Agent-051 — REFERRAL ACTIVATION AGENT

**Phase:** 4 — Retention & Revenue
**Timeline:** Days 7–14
**Committee:** Revenue
**Priority:** HIGH

---

## Mission

Activate the referral system built by Agent-002. Drive referral usage through in-app prompts, email campaigns, and milestone rewards. Target: 200+ referral signups by Day 14.

## Tasks

1. **In-App Referral Prompts**:
   - Post-first-value moment: "Love Veklom? Share with a friend and earn $10"
   - Post-purchase: "Share your experience — give $10, get $10"
   - Dashboard widget showing referral stats
   - Milestone celebrations (5 referrals, 10 referrals, 25 referrals)
2. **Referral Email Campaign** (coordinate with Agent-052):
   - "Your referral link is ready" — Day 1 after signup
   - "You're 3 referrals away from Gold status" — milestone nudge
   - "Your friend just signed up!" — reward notification
   - Weekly referral leaderboard email
3. **Milestone Rewards Program**:
   - 3 referrals: Bronze (extra 500 wallet credits)
   - 5 referrals: Silver (1 month Pro free)
   - 10 referrals: Gold (3 months Pro free + "Gold Member" badge)
   - 25 referrals: Platinum (lifetime Pro + swag)
4. **Referral Leaderboard**:
   - Public leaderboard page showing top referrers
   - Monthly prizes for top 3 referrers
5. **Tracking & Attribution**:
   - UTM tracking on all referral links
   - Referral funnel analytics (sent → clicked → signed up → converted)
   - A/B test referral messaging

## Success Metrics

| Metric | Target |
|---|---|

| Referral conversion (signup → paid) | > 10% |
