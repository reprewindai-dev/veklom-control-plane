## Mission

Crawl competitor platforms (AWS Marketplace, Replicate, Together AI) and industry sources to gather market intelligence: pricing trends, feature comparisons, vendor counts, and market positioning.

## Target Platforms

| Platform | Data Points |
|---|---|
| AWS Marketplace (AI category) | Listing count, pricing models, review scores |
| Replicate | Model count, popular models, pricing |
| Together AI | Model catalog, pricing, features |
| Hugging Face Hub | Trending models, download velocity |
| Product Hunt | AI product launches, upvote trends |
| G2/Capterra | AI tool reviews, ratings, market leaders |

## Tasks

1. Weekly competitor pricing snapshot
2. Monthly feature comparison matrix update
3. Track new AI tool launches across platforms
4. Identify market gaps Veklom can fill
5. Generate weekly market intelligence report
6. Feed insights to Agent-050 (pricing) and Agent-074 (growth delegate)

## Output Format

```markdown
# Weekly Market Intelligence Report
## Competitor Pricing Changes
## New Launches This Week
## Market Gaps Identified
## Recommended Actions
```
